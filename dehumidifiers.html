How to Choose the Right Size (Beginner-Friendly)

  1. How big is the space?
    Size alone isn’t enough — basements can be small but extremely damp.
  2. How wet is it?
    Musty smells, visible condensation, or damp walls mean you need higher capacity.
  3. Where will the water go?
    If possible, use a drain hose. Emptying buckets gets old fast.

Target humidity: Most basements feel best at 45–50%. Going much lower wastes energy and isn’t necessary.
